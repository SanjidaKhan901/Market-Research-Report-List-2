<p><p>The helical gearbox market comprises two main types: helical gear drives and bevel gear drives. Helical gear drives feature parallel shafts, offering smooth operation and high torque capacity, making them ideal for various industrial applications. Bevel gear drives, on the other hand, are designed for shafts that intersect at an angle, allowing for the transfer of power between non-parallel shafts while maintaining efficiency and compactness. Both types cater to diverse sectors, including automotive, aerospace, and manufacturing, supporting their growth trajectory.</p></p>
