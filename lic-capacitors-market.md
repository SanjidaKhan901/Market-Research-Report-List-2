<p><p>The LIC capacitors market comprises primarily two types: radial and laminating capacitors. Radial type capacitors feature leads extending in a single direction, making them suitable for compact designs in electronic devices. They are commonly used in power supply applications. Laminating type capacitors, on the other hand, consist of multiple layers of dielectric material, enhancing their performance in high-frequency applications. This type is often favored in environments requiring high stability and reliability, catering to various sectors including automotive and industrial electronics.</p></p>
