<p><p>The underwater research side scan sonar market is divided into two main types: single-beam and multi-beam sonar systems. Single-beam sonar is characterized by a narrow sound wave focus, providing high-resolution images of the seafloor over a limited area, making it ideal for specific target identification. In contrast, multi-beam sonar emits multiple sound beams simultaneously, allowing for broader area coverage and detailed mapping of underwater terrains. This technology is essential for applications such as marine archaeology, habitat mapping, and underwater surveys.</p></p>
