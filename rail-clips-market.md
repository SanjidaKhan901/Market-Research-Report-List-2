<p><ul><li>Weldable Clip</li><li>Boltable Clip</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The rail clips market is primarily divided into two types: weldable clips and boltable clips. Weldable clips are permanently attached to the rail using welding techniques, providing strong and fixed connections, ideal for high-speed rail applications. In contrast, boltable clips use bolts for fastening, allowing for easier installation and maintenance, as they can be removed and reattached. Both types play crucial roles in ensuring track stability and safety, catering to different requirements in rail infrastructure projects.</p></p>
