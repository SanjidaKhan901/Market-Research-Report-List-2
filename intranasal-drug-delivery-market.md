<p><p>Intranasal drug delivery is gaining traction due to its convenience and rapid absorption. The market is typically segmented into multidose and uni/bidose systems. Multidose devices allow multiple applications from a single container, enhancing user convenience and cost-effectiveness. In contrast, uni/bidose devices deliver a single or two doses per application, often improving precision and reducing contamination risks. Both types cater to different therapeutic needs and patient preferences, influencing their adoption in various clinical settings.</p></p>
