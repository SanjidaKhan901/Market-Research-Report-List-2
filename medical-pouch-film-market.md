<p><p>The Medical Pouch Film market encompasses various types of films used for packaging medical products. Aluminum foil offers excellent barrier properties, protecting contents from moisture and light. BOPP (Biaxially-oriented polypropylene) film is lightweight and flexible, ideal for sterile packaging. BOPA (Biaxially-oriented polyamide) film provides high strength and puncture resistance. BOPET (Biaxially-oriented polyethylene terephthalate) film is durable and has good clarity. CPP (Cast polypropylene) film is suitable for heat sealing, while LLDPE (Linear low-density polyethylene) film offers elasticity and toughness. Other films include specialized materials tailored for specific medical applications.</p></p>
