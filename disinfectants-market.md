<p><strong>The Disinfectants Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Oxidising Disinfectants</li><li>Non-oxidising Disinfectants</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The disinfectants market is primarily divided into two types: oxidizing and non-oxidizing disinfectants. Oxidizing disinfectants, such as chlorine and hydrogen peroxide, disrupt microbial cell structures through oxidation, effectively killing bacteria, viruses, and fungi. Non-oxidizing disinfectants, like quaternary ammonium compounds and phenolic compounds, work by interfering with microbial metabolism and cell function without oxidation. Both types are widely used across various sectors including healthcare, food processing, and water treatment, each offering unique advantages based on their properties and applications.</p></p>
