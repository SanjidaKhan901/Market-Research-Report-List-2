<p><ul><li>Synchronous</li><li>Asynchronous</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The DC servomotor market is primarily divided into synchronous and asynchronous types. Synchronous servomotors operate at constant speed, maintaining a fixed relationship with the input signal, ideal for precise control applications. Asynchronous servomotors, on the other hand, operate at varying speeds and can provide higher torque at lower speeds, making them suitable for applications requiring flexibility. Both types are crucial for industries like robotics, automation, and aerospace, where accurate motion control and performance are essential.</p></p>
