<p><p>The Adaptive Optics Components Market encompasses several key types. Wavefront sensors detect and analyze wavefront distortions, crucial for correcting optical aberrations. Wavefront modulators actively adjust the wavefront through devices like deformable mirrors. The control system interprets data from the sensors, coordinating adjustments in real-time to optimize imaging quality. Additionally, the "Other" market segment includes auxiliary components and software that support adaptive optics applications in fields such as astronomy, telecommunications, and medical imaging, enhancing overall system performance.</p></p>
