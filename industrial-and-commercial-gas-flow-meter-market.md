<p><p>The Industrial and Commercial Gas Flow Meter market includes various technologies, primarily the diaphragm gas flow meter and the rotary gas flow meter. Diaphragm gas flow meters utilize a flexible diaphragm to measure gas flow by responding to pressure changes, making them suitable for low to medium flow rates. On the other hand, rotary gas flow meters employ rotating elements to provide accurate measurements across a wider range of flow rates, often preferred in high-accuracy applications. Both types are essential for efficient gas management in various industries.</p></p>
