<p><p>Moving walks, also known as travelators, are categorized into two main types: horizontal and inclined. Horizontal moving walks operate on a flat surface, providing seamless transportation in airports, shopping malls, and public spaces, enhancing pedestrian flow and reducing travel time. Inclined moving walks, on the other hand, are designed to operate on a slope, ideal for transporting people between different levels, such as in hilly areas or multi-story buildings. Both types improve accessibility and comfort for users.</p></p>
