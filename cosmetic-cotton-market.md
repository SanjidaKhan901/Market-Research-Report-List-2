<p><p>The cosmetic cotton market encompasses two primary types: absorbent cotton and non-woven fabrics. Absorbent cotton is highly porous and designed for maximum moisture retention, making it ideal for skincare applications and makeup removal. Non-woven fabrics, on the other hand, are produced by bonding fibers together through mechanical or chemical processes, offering durability and versatility for various cosmetic uses, such as wipes and pads. Both types cater to consumer needs for hygiene and convenience in beauty routines.</p></p>
