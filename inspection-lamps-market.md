<p><p>The inspection lamps market is primarily divided into two types: magnetic and non-magnetic. Magnetic inspection lamps utilize strong magnets for easy attachment to metallic surfaces, allowing hands-free operation during inspections. They are ideal for automotive and industrial applications. On the other hand, non-magnetic inspection lamps provide versatility in environments where magnets cannot be used, offering portability and flexibility for various settings. Both types cater to different user needs depending on the work environment and specific inspection requirements.</p></p>
