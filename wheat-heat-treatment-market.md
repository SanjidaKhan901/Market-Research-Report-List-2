<p><p>The Wheat Heat Treatment Market is segmented into two primary types: dry treatment and wet treatment. Dry treatment involves the application of heat to dry wheat, enhancing its shelf life and preventing pest infestations without moisture involvement. In contrast, wet treatment combines heat with moisture, often used for specific processing needs like gelatinization or improving dough performance. Both methods aim to enhance wheat quality, ensuring better storage and processing outcomes in the food industry.</p></p>
