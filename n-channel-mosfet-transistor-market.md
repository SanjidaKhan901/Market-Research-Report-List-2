<p><p>The N-channel MOSFET transistor market is primarily segmented into enhancement-type and depletion-type devices. Enhancement-type MOSFETs are designed to conduct when a voltage is applied to the gate, making them widely used in digital circuits and power applications for their high efficiency. In contrast, depletion-type MOSFETs are normally on and require a negative gate voltage to turn off, which makes them suitable for applications needing constant current control. Both types offer unique advantages, driving demand across various electronic sectors.</p></p>
