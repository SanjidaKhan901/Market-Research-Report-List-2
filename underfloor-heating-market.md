<p><strong>The Underfloor Heating Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Hydronic Heating</li><li>Electric Heating</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Underfloor heating systems primarily fall into two categories: hydronic heating and electric heating. Hydronic heating circulates warm water through pipes laid beneath the floor, providing consistent warmth and energy efficiency, making it ideal for larger areas. In contrast, electric heating employs electric cables or mats installed under the flooring, offering faster installation and heating, suitable for smaller spaces and renovations. Both types enhance comfort, improve energy efficiency, and can be integrated with various flooring materials, catering to diverse consumer needs.</p></p>
