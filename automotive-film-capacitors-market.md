<p><p>The automotive film capacitors market encompasses various types, including PET, PEN, PP, and PPS capacitors. PET capacitors are favored for their excellent temperature stability and reliability. PEN capacitors offer higher dielectric strength and are used in demanding environments. PP capacitors are known for low losses and high efficiency, making them suitable for coupling and decoupling applications. PPS capacitors provide thermal stability and excellent performance in high-frequency applications, making them ideal for advanced automotive electronics. Each type serves specific needs within the automotive sector.</p></p>
