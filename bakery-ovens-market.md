<p><ul><li>Convection Oven</li><li>Deck Oven</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The bakery ovens market features two primary types: convection ovens and deck ovens. Convection ovens utilize fans to circulate hot air, ensuring even baking and quicker cooking times, making them ideal for pastries and cookies. In contrast, deck ovens consist of several baking chambers with stone or brick surfaces, providing robust heat retention and superior crust quality for artisan bread and pizzas. Both oven types serve distinct baking needs, catering to various production scales and baking techniques in the industry.</p></p>
