<p><p>Water fire extinguishers are categorized into two main types: portable and cart-mounted. In contrast, cart-type extinguishers are larger and mounted on wheels, making them suitable for more extensive fire coverage and transportation in industrial settings. These cart-mounted models can hold larger volumes, beneficial for managing bigger fires effectively.</p></p>
